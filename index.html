Who is Charles?

I’m a Computer Programming student at Columbia College Chicago with experience in sales, business operations, and software development.

I’ve worked in tax preparation, where I handled client communication, Excel systems, QuickBooks, and business filings, and I currently work in sales where I focus on communication, problem-solving, and closing.

On the technical side, I build applications that solve real problems, including tools that integrate with platforms like Canvas, automate workflows, and manage structured data.

I’m especially interested in combining technology with real-world systems — building tools that actually get used, not just projects that sit on GitHub.
